What Is Cosmic Time?

Cosmic time is a concept used in cosmology to describe the age and evolution of the universe from the Big Bang to the present moment.

It provides a unified way to measure time on the largest possible scale.

Why Time Zones Exist

Time zones were introduced to synchronize clocks across different regions of the Earth as global communication and transportation evolved.

Why Space Exploration Matters
