What Is Cosmic Time?

Cosmic time is a concept used in cosmology to describe the age and evolution of the universe from the Big Bang to the present moment. It provides a unified timeline for understanding how the universe has changed over billions of years.

Why Space Exploration Matters

Space exploration expands human knowledge, drives technological innovation, and helps us better understand our place in the universe. Many modern technologies originated from space research.
