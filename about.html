About the Project

CosmoTime is an independent informational and educational project dedicated to time, space, astronomy, and science.

The project combines a practical world clock tool with educational content designed to explain complex topics such as time zones, global synchronization, cosmic time, and space exploration.

Our mission is to make scientific knowledge more accessible and understandable for a wide audience through clear explanations and carefully curated materials.

All content on this website is published strictly for educational and informational purposes.
